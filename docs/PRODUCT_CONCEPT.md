# Bean & Brew: Product Concept

## 1. Business Context & Objective
The client is transitioning from local farmers' markets and wholesale to a Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) online model.
**Primary Objective:** Digitalize the artisanal brand experience while maintaining simplicity and authenticity for non-technical founders.

## 2. Product Vision
The digital storefront must act as a "virtual shelf" that reflects the physical roastery. Focus is on visual clarity, coffee origin storytelling, and a frictionless purchase flow.

## 3. Road-to-Market (BMAD Strategy)
We follow a phased approach to ensure stability and validated learning.

### Phase 1: The Digital Shelf (CURRENT)
- **High-Performance Infrastructure:** Next.js 15, Neon PostgreSQL, and Tailwind CSS.
- **Boutique Catalog:** A clean, curated display of specialty roasts with origin, process, and flavor profile data.
- **Artisanal UI:** Minimalist design, premium typography, and mobile-first responsiveness.

### Phase 2: AI Sommelier (PLANNED)
- **AI Integration:** Implementing a conversational layer (Claude API) to guide non-expert customers.
- **Personalized Recommendations:** Replicating the "market-stall conversation" where founders help customers find their perfect bean.

## 4. MVP Core Requirements
- **Performance:** Sub-1s page loads for high conversion.
- **Simplicity:** No complex filters in V1; focus on discovery.
- **Scalability:** Robust data schema ready for inventory expansion.
